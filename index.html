Welcome to the Meteor Shower Page!

Meteor Shower Page is a cool App about meteorite strikes. In the Meteor Shower Page you feel excited about space and our planet. Our Page will help you understand meteorites better and share ideas with others around the world. Meteor Shower Page will let curious people explore space stuff and history. With fun features, you can learn about meteorites and how they hit Earth. We used easy Search tools to help you look at the data, see cool charts, and know where the meteorites hit.

Toolbar Search

You can search what you have in your mind in the meteorite strikes area.

Type here in search box and we will show you all data that we have in our DataBase related to your typed content: you can Search: Date, Number, Words

Advanced Search

In Meteor Shower search component you are able to filter your search by selecting fields in the search component.

  • Fields for data are:
    • Name
    • Year of strike
    • Meteorite composition (recclass)
    • Mass range (e.g. low to high, inclusive)
    • Location

More Options

You have the ability to save your frequently used search criteria, so that you can easily reselect them from a dropdown without having to manually enter them each time.

You also have the ability to see the result of your search in rows and on a map.

You have the ability to sort the data displayed in ascending or descending sequence. So that you can see the data more clearly.

You have the access to definitions of the data contained in each column of the table

So that you can understand quickly the meaning of the info without searching on other sites.

You have the ability to see other results displaying if there is not any result according to your input, or find similar results. The results should be like: Total number of strikes, Average mass, Histogram showing, number of strikes by year, Histogram showing number of strikes by meteorite composition (recclass).
