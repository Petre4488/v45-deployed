Welcome to Meteor Shower!

Meteor Shower, your gateway to exploring the captivating world of meteorite strikes on Earth. This interactive web application allows you to delve into the vast collection of meteorite strike data maintained by NASA. Immerse yourself in the mysteries of space and time as you uncover insights, analyze trends, and discover the incredible stories behind these cosmic events.

Our Mission

At Meteor Shower, our mission is to make the wonders of the universe accessible to everyone. We believe that every meteorite strike has a unique tale to tell, and by presenting this data in an intuitive and informative way, we aim to inspire curiosity, spark conversations, and foster a deeper appreciation for the celestial phenomena that have shaped our planet's history.

Features

Search and Filter

Dive into the data using various search criteria such as name, year of strike, meteorite composition, and mass range. Tailor your exploration to your interests.

Interactive Visualizations

Experience meteorite strikes in a whole new light with visually engaging histograms showcasing strike frequencies by year and meteorite composition.

Summary Metrics

Gain insights into the data with summary metrics like the total number of strikes and the average mass. These metrics provide a snapshot of the data's characteristics.

Customization

Make Meteor Shower your own by choosing between dark and light modes and adjusting font preferences to suit your viewing experience.

Join the Cosmic Journey

Whether you're a space enthusiast, a data explorer, or just someone curious about the celestial events that have shaped our world, Meteor Shower welcomes you to embark on a cosmic journey like no other. The universe is vast, and every meteorite holds a piece of its history. Are you ready to uncover the secrets of the stars?
